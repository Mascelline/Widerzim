Welcome to WIDER-ZIMBABWE

The Women’s Institute for Development,Education and Research-Zimbabwe(WIDER-Zim) partners a diversity of stakeholders, at various levels, to foster sustainable, community centred and driven development; advance education, promote dialogue on pertinent issues and engage in policy oriented research.Departing from a gender-sensitive human rights based approach that locates girls and women at the centre of all programming , WIDER-Zimbabwe is guided by local national development priorities entrenched within the broader, global sustainable development agenda. While we have a national programming outlook, it is the under-served, often hard to reach rural schools and their surrounding communities that are at the heart of WIDER-Zimbabwe interventions.

Vision

Equitable, Self Sustainable Communities in which girls and women realise their full potential.
